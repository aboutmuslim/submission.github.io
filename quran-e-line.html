بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

Bismillah hir rahman nir raheem

In the name of Allah, the Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful.

Translation:   sahih International

Aayat:   1

ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ

Alhamdu lillaahi Rabbil ‘aalameen

[All] praise is [due] to Allah, Lord of the worlds

Translation:   sahih International

Aayat:   2

ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

Ar-Rahmaanir-Raheem

The Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful,

Translation:   sahih International

Aayat:   3

مَـٰلِكِ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ

Maaliki Yawmid-Deen

Sovereign of the Day of Recompense.

Translation:   sahih International

Aayat:   4

إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

Iyyaaka na’budu wa lyyaaka nasta’een

It is You we worship and You we ask for help.

Translation:   sahih International

Aayat:   5

ٱهْدِنَا ٱلصِّرَٰطَ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمَ

Ihdinas-Siraatal-Mustaqeem

Guide us to the straight path –

Translation:   sahih International

Aayat:   6

صِرَٰطَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ

Siraatal-lazeena an’amta ‘alaihim

The path of those upon whom You have bestowed favor

Translation:   sahih International

Aayat:   7

غَيْرِ ٱلْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا ٱلضَّآلِّينَ

Ghayril-maghdoobi ‘alaihim wa lad-daaalleen

not of those who have evoked [Your] anger or of those who are astray.

Translation:   sahih International
